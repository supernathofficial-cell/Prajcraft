Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with Prajcraft. This Shipping Policy explains how we process and deliver your orders.

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed after successful payment confirmation. Processing generally begins within 24–48 business hours.

2. Shipping Time

Orders are shipped through registered domestic courier services or Speed Post. Most orders are dispatched within 7 business days from the date of order or payment confirmation.

3. Delivery Time

Delivery times depend on your location, courier availability, and other operational factors. Estimated delivery dates are provided for reference only and are not guaranteed.

4. Shipping Partners

We use trusted courier companies and postal services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders.

5. Delivery Address

Orders will be delivered to the shipping address provided by the customer during checkout. Please ensure that all address details are accurate to avoid delivery delays.

6. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are displayed during checkout. Any shipping fees paid are non-refundable unless otherwise required by law.

7. Delayed Deliveries

While we make every effort to deliver your order on time, we are not responsible for delays caused by courier companies, postal services, natural disasters, strikes, or other circumstances beyond our control.

8. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, tracking information may be shared via email or SMS, where available.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding shipping or delivery, please contact our customer support using the contact details available on our website.

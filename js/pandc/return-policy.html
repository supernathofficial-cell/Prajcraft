Return Policy

At Prajcraft, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return or exchange according to the following policy.

1. Return Period

Products are eligible for return or exchange within 10 days from the date of delivery. Requests made after this period may not be accepted.

2. Return Eligibility

To qualify for a return, the product must:

  • Be unused and in its original condition.
  • Include all original packaging and accessories.
  • Contain all tags, labels, and invoices.
  • Not be damaged by misuse or negligence.

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized products.
  • Products purchased during clearance or special sale (where applicable).
  • Items marked as non-returnable.
  • Products damaged after delivery due to improper handling.

4. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please notify our customer support immediately. After verification, we may approve a replacement, exchange, or refund.

5. Exchange Policy

Products are exchanged only if they are received damaged, defective, or different from the item ordered. Replacement is subject to product availability.

6. Return Process

  • Contact our customer support team.
  • Provide your Order ID and product details.
  • Share photographs if requested.
  • Wait for return approval.
  • Ship the product as instructed.

7. Inspection

Once the returned item is received, it will be inspected. If approved, your exchange or refund will be processed according to our policies.

8. Refund After Return

Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider.

9. Contact Us

For any return or exchange request, please contact our customer support through the contact information available on our website.

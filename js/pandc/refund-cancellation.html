Refund & Cancellation Policy

Thank you for shopping with Prajcraft. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the conditions under which orders may be cancelled and refunds may be processed.

1. Order Cancellation

Cancellation requests will only be accepted within 2 days of placing the order.

Once an order has been processed for shipping or dispatched by the seller, it cannot be cancelled. In such cases, customers may refuse delivery if applicable.

2. Non-Cancellable Products

  • Customized or personalized products.
  • Perishable goods.
  • Products already shipped.
  • Products marked as non-cancellable.

3. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please contact our customer support within 2 days of delivery.

Our team may request photographs or additional information before approving a replacement or refund.

4. Incorrect Product

If the delivered product does not match your order, please report the issue within 2 days of receiving it. We will investigate the matter and provide an appropriate resolution.

5. Manufacturer Warranty

Products covered by a manufacturer's warranty should be serviced directly through the manufacturer according to their warranty terms.

6. Refund Process

  • Refunds are initiated only after approval.
  • Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method.
  • Refunds may take up to 10 business days to reflect.

7. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests may be rejected if:

  • The product has been used or damaged by the customer.
  • The request is made after the allowed period.
  • The product falls under a non-refundable category.
  • Required proof is not provided.

8. Contact Us

For refund or cancellation requests, please contact our customer support using the contact details available on our website.
